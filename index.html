How it works

Your action center, explained.

📝 Notes

Three tabs: Office, Personal, Home. Say "Costco", "groceries", "Amazon" or any food/shopping word and the note auto-routes to your Personal Grocery section. You can also speak notes with the mic — review in the text box, then tap Add.

✨ Today

Your most important task is always at the top. Tap Open / In Progress / Done to update status. Swipe right = done, swipe left = snooze.

🎤 Capture

Tap the mic and speak your task (works in Safari). Say "urgent" → auto high priority. Or just type it.

🪄 Voice finish

Tap "What did you finish?" and say what you completed. It matches and checks them off automatically.

🔔 Notifications

Enable reminders on the home screen. You'll get nudged every 6 hours for pending tasks.

🎁 Send a Gift

Pick an occasion, generate a heartfelt AI message, send via SMS, or open Amazon to send a gift card directly.

💝 Important Dates

Add birthdays, anniversaries, plans. A banner appears on your home screen when a date is coming up soon.

👨‍👩‍👧 Share with Family

Save phone numbers for Piyush and Kiara in the Family screen. Then tap "📤 Share" on any date to send them a text with a one-tap link to add it to their calendar.
